Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free?

Yes — no credit card required. You get 5 AI generations per hour and up to 3 projects.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your billing portal. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods are accepted?

All major credit/debit cards via Stripe. Secure, encrypted payments.

Do I own the generated code?

Yes — all generated code is yours to use, modify, and deploy commercially.

Is there a dev/test mode?

Yes — without Stripe keys configured, upgrades work instantly in dev mode for testing.

What's the API access?

Business plan users get API keys to integrate WebGen into their own apps via REST API.
